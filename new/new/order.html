More info about Grace Peters Bakery and Catering

Grace Catering and Bakery services specialises in providing ethnic nigerian and west african food to it's cient, staying true to its root making sure that even the ingrediants used in cooking are imported from Nigeria to retain the original quality of Nigerian food. Grace is a very flexible cook and is willing to customize any order is the proper negotiations are set. Grace woud also prefer to provide her catering service in very large portions. Grace has proven to be a diverse cook and is open to general request, an overall motherly customer service.
